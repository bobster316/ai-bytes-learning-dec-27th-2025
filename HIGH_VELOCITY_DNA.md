# 🧬 High-Velocity DNA: The Learning Algorithm

This document codifies the internal standards for all content generated or created for the AI Bytes Learning Platform.

## 1. The 15-Minute Constraint
We do not sell 40-hour lectures. We sell **15-minute breakthroughs**.
- **Lesson Length**: 800 - 1000 words.
- **Topic Length**: 150 - 300 words.
- **Course Duration**: Exactly 60 minutes for a standard track.

## 2. The Metaphor-First Anchor
Complexity is the enemy of learning. We anchor every technical concept in a familiar mental model.
- **Rule**: Never lead with a definition. Lead with a story or comparison.
- **Persona Alignment**: Sterling uses these metaphors with sophisticated clarity.

## 3. The Rule of 4 (Visuals)
Every lesson must achieve visual integrity through four specific stages:
1. **THE HOOK**: A visual metaphor that makes the problem "felt".
2. **THE ENGINE**: A technical diagram/visualization of how it works (under the hood).
3. **THE PROOF**: A real-world application/case study visual.
4. **THE RECAP**: A high-density summary visual for memory retention.

## 4. Outcome-First Engineering
Every lesson must answer: **"What can I do now that I couldn't do 15 minutes ago?"**
- **The "Try This" Action**: A 5-minute practical task included at the end of every Byte.

## 5. Sterling's Voice (Character Pillars)
- **Sophisticated & Dry**: Academically smug but always helpful.
- **British RP**: The "Professor" persona.
- **Efficient**: No preamble. No internal monologue. Just high-velocity insight.
