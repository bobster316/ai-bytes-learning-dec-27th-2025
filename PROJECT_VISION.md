# AI Bytes Learning: Project Vision & DNA

## 🧬 The Core DNA: High-Velocity Learning
AI Bytes Learning is not just an e-learning platform; it is a **Velocity Engine**. We believe that anyone can master AI if it is presented with clarity, speed, and structural integrity.

### 1. The 15-Minute Byte
Legacy education is bloated. We condense 1 hour of traditional learning into 15 minutes of high-intensity "Bytes". 
- **Constraint**: No lesson ever exceeds 1000 words.
- **Goal**: Immediate practical application (The "5-Minute Win").

### 2. Metaphor-First Pedagogy
We strip away the academic jargon. Every complex AI concept must be anchored in an everyday metaphor before the technical "Engine" is explained.
- *Example*: Explaining Neural Networks through the lens of a "Decision-making Committee".

### 3. The Rule of 4 (Visual Integrity)
Visuals are not decorative; they are cognitive anchors. Every lesson must feature exactly 4 visuals:
1. **The Hook**: Visualises the problem/analogy.
2. **The Engine**: Visualises the technical mechanism.
3. **The Proof**: Visualises a real-world result.
4. **The Recap**: A high-speed visual summary.

### 4. Sterling: The Sophisticated Coach
Our AI, Sterling, is the guardian of this DNA. He is designed to be:
- **Efficient**: He despises rambling.
- **Authoritative**: He is an expert in the "Planetary Engine" of AI.
- **British (RP)**: Sophisticated, dry, and professional.

## 🗣 Human-First Communication Standard
We have a zero-tolerance policy for "AI-isms" (marketing fluff generated by LLMs). All copy must be grounded, specific, and human.

- **Banned Words**: Mastery, Unleash, Elevate, Realm, Landscape, Tapestry.
- **Approved Voice**: Practical, Direct, Specific. (e.g., "Build Tools" instead of "Unleash Potential").
- **Reference**: See `CONTENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md` for the full list of banned terms and approved alternatives.
