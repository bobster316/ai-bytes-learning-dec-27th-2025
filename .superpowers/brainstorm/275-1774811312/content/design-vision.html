Variation Architecture

Three independent levels. Each amplifies the last.

Course DNA sets the universe. Module DNA sets the chapter. Lesson DNA sets the moment. The same concept taught in lesson 3 of course A feels completely different from lesson 3 of course B — and from lesson 4 of the same course.

Never-Before-Done

Six experiences that don't exist anywhere else.

Beyond blocks. These are platform-level experiences that no e-learning platform — Brilliant, MasterClass, Duolingo, Khan Academy — has ever shipped.

01

Cross-Lesson Memory

Time Capsule blocks create predictions that surface in future lessons. The platform remembers what you thought at lesson 2 and confronts you with it at lesson 8. Narrative arc across an entire course.

02

Live Concept Graph

Neural Map builds in real-time as you read. By the end of every lesson, you can see exactly how all the ideas connect as an animated node network. Understanding becomes visible.

03

Enforced Reflection Gate

Signal Interrupt blocks stop progress mid-lesson and demand typed reflection before the next block unlocks. The only platform that makes comprehension a physical act, not a passive assumption.

04

Lesson Visual Personality

Each lesson has an emotional tone — Electric for high-stakes topics, Calm for foundational concepts, Cinematic for case studies. The UI's entire character shifts to match the content's energy.

05

Module-Level DNA

The first platform where not just courses, but individual modules have their own visual identity — hue-shifted from the course palette, with their own header style and progress display. Three nested design systems.

06

Micro-Variation Seeding

Tiny seeded differences in border-radius, glow intensity, and spacing offsets make every lesson feel hand-designed even though it's generated. The platform looks crafted, not templated.

Motion Language

Every transition earns its place.

Animations are not decoration — each motion pattern communicates something. The platform has a defined motion vocabulary that varies by archetype, never uses generic easing, and serves the pedagogy.

🌊
Semantic Reveal
Blocks reveal in the direction knowledge flows — left-to-right for process, top-to-bottom for hierarchy, radial for concepts.
⚡
Kinetic Typography
Key terms animate their entry with weight and impact. Variable font width compresses on entry, expands to full. Text has velocity.
🌀
Archetype Easing
Bold archetype uses spring physics. Clinical uses linear precision. Warm uses ease-in-out curves. Motion matches personality.
🔬
Micro-Choreography
Staggered delays between related elements (0.04s per child). Grid items don't all appear at once — they cascade in formation.
🌑
Context Transition
Moving from module to module: a brief dark-field moment — the old context fades, new module DNA breathes in. Like a film cut.
