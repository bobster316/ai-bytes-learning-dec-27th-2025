New Block Types — Select What Excites You

Pick as many as you want. These are the "wow" blocks that top e-learning platforms don't have.

🎮 Interactive / Gamified
1

Branching Scenario

Choose-your-own-path decisions. "You're the AI engineer — what do you do?" Multiple outcomes, consequences shown.

2

Drag & Rank

Drag items into the correct order or category. "Rank these AI models by capability" — tactile, memorable.

3

Hotspot Image

Click areas of a diagram to reveal explanations. Perfect for architecture diagrams, neural network layers, workflows.

4

Mid-Lesson Knowledge Check

A fast 1-question pulse check mid-paragraph. "Before we go further — what do you think this means?" Non-graded, instant.

📊 Data & Visual
5

Animated Stat Block

Big numbers that count up on scroll. "GPT-4 has 1.76 trillion parameters" — the number animates from 0. Visceral impact.

6

Before / After Slider

Drag a divider between two states. "AI output vs human output", "Before fine-tuning vs after". Interactive comparison.

7

Animated Chart

Bar/line/radar charts that animate in on scroll. Shows AI benchmarks, adoption curves, cost trends — not static images.

8

Interactive Timeline

Scroll through a horizontal or vertical timeline. "History of AI" — each era clicks open with detail. Cinematic scroll reveal.

🎬 Cinematic / Immersive
9

Scroll-Driven Narrative

Content reveals as you scroll — text fades in, diagrams build step by step. The "Apple product page" effect.

10

Split Screen Reveal

Left side: concept. Right side: real-world application. Animated split that dramatises the connection.

11

Cinematic Code Reveal

Code that types itself out with syntax highlighting, line by line. Explains what each section does as it appears.

12

Expert Quote Wall

3–4 industry expert quotes in a magazine-style mosaic. Photos, names, companies. Adds credibility and visual richness.

🧠 Deep Learning
13

Reflection Prompt

Open-ended "pause and think" moment. "How would this apply to your job?" — text area, stored to profile, revisitable.

14

Myth Buster

"Myth vs Reality" reveal card. Learner sees a common misconception, guesses true/false, then the truth explodes onto screen.

15

Perspective Toggle

Same topic explained from 3 viewpoints — e.g. "For a developer / For a CEO / For a regulator". Tabbed, instant switch.

16

Interactive Concept Map

A node graph of how concepts connect. Click a node to zoom in. Shows "where this lesson fits" in the bigger picture.
